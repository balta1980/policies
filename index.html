Privacy Policy for Scorer

Last Updated: April 23, 2026

1. Introduction This privacy policy applies to the Scorer mobile application (herein referred to as "the Application"). The Application is designed to help users track scores for card and domino games.

2. Data Collection and Use The Application does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information from its users.

  • Personal Data: We do not ask for names, emails, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.

  • Persistent Data: The Application uses local storage on your device to keep track of game scores and settings (persistent variables). This data is stored locally and stays on your device at all times.

3. Permissions The Application does not require or request any special device permissions, such as access to your location, camera, contacts, or microphone.

4. Third-Party Services and Internet Access The Application does not have internet access and does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services. Your data is never shared with, sold to, or accessible by any third party.

5. Data Retention All game data is stored locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time by clearing the Application's cache or uninstalling the Application from your device.

6. Children’s Privacy Since the Application does not collect any personal data, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect user information, we cannot notify you personally; therefore, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
